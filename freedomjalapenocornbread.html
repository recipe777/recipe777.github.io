Jalapeno Cornbread

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups cornmeal
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2/3 Cup oil
  • 1 Cup Sour Cream
  • 1 Cup frozen corn
  • 3 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3-5 chopped Jalapeno peppers
  • 1 Cup grated cheese

Recipe

Mix all ingredients together. Pour in greased pan. Bake at 350 for 45min.
