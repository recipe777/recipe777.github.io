Egg in the Hole

Ingredients

  • One slice of bread with hole cut out in center
  • butter
  • egg

Recipe

Butter the slice of bread on both sides and begin to brown the first side in a skillet. While the first side is grilling, crack egg into "hole". When the bread is browned on side #1 flip to other side and continue to brown and cook egg to desired consistency. Serve on plate back to side one. It will look the best.
